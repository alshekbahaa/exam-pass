FAQ

Questions, answered simply.

A straightforward study companion that keeps the focus on preparation and genuine progress.

Who is Exam-Pass for?

It is designed for students who want more structure, focused practice, and confidence as an exam approaches.

Does it replace a teacher or tutor?

No. Exam-Pass supports personal preparation. It can complement lessons, tutoring, and the materials provided by an educational institution.

Can it help with last-minute revision?

Yes. A clear review plan and focused practice can help prioritize remaining study time responsibly.

What does ethical preparation mean?

It means learning the material and preparing honestly for an exam — no shortcuts that undermine academic integrity.
